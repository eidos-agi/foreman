# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Foreman to be useful, direct, and humane for contributors building
agentic software. We commit to a respectful project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, kind, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming risks plainly.
- Keep criticism focused on the work and its impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately derailing technical review or release safety work.

## Enforcement

Report issues to daniel@eidosagi.com. Maintainers may remove comments, close
threads, or block contributors when needed to protect the project.
